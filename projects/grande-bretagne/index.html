Grande Bretagne: picture lights refurbishment

  Hospitality, Specialized
Home › Projects

Hotel Grande Bretagne is a landmark hotel situated right in the heart of the city of Athens since 1874. Ηundreds of works of art housed in its walls.

L4A took over the refurbishment of the traditional picture lights by replacing the halogen lamps with LED’s with excellent color rendering properties.
