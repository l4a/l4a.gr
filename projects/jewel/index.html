A jewel made in Greece

Filed under: Cultural

The design of the exhibition intented to a harmonious coexistense of 26 artists.

The composition of three display units for each artist is the key element. Its repetition defines the overall design system, imparting consistency and homogeneity in space. The basic lighting unit is a part of the composition suppling a distinct lighting level. It consists of 3 hanging lamps at different heights for each key element.
