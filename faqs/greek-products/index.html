All the manufacturers you work with are foreign. Do you have some problem with Greek products?

Home › FAQs ›

None whatsoever! On the contrary, we are pleased to see quality products made by Greek hands. However, as is the case with most sectors of industrial production, the truth is that Greek lighting manufacturers invest little in fields such as research, innovation and design. That doesn’t mean that there aren’t specific products from specific Greek firms that we use.
