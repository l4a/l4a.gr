On the boards VII

Filed under: On-the-boards, Lighting-simulation

The ultimate challenge for the involved in the lighting design process is the quick & reliable imaging of the ideas without the hassle of using sophisticated, expensive and time consuming software like 3dstudio, Rhino, Maya etc.

The first image on the left was produced by the humble Relux and its internal raytracer. It took just 3-4 hours, starting from scratch, to finish and most of the time spent on adjusting the raytracer settings. Of course it’s not the best result on planet earth but it’s ok for everyday’s work and surely captures the real atmosphere of the space. Have in mind that this certain case (indirect lighitng + artificial skylight) is tough enough for every lighting software.
