Royal Botania

We are excited to announce the beginning of our exclusive partnership with Royal Botania regarding its Outdoor Lighting Collection.

Royal Botania uses superior natural materials: premium Javanese plantation teak, granite, stainless steel and glass. When placed outside all the Royal Botania lighting models integrate seamlessly into their natural environment. Most of the lamps are designed to use energy-saving light bulbs with low energy consumption or LEDs.
