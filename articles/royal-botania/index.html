Royal Botania

Filed under: News

We are excited to announce the beginning of our exclusive partnership with Royal Botania regarding its Outdoor Lighting Collection.

Royal Botania uses superior natural materials: premium Javanese plantation teak, granite, stainless steel, glass. When placed outside all the Royal Botania lighting models will integrate into their natural environment. Most of the lamps are designed to use energy-saving light bulbs with low energy consumption and LED.
