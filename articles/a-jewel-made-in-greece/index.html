“A jewel made in Greece” opening

The exhibition A Jewel Made in Greece opening on Saturday, 22 February 2014, at Technopolis, brings most of the greatest modern Greek jewellery artists together under the same roof for about one week.

The event was organised by Mary Samoli, visual designer, who came up with the concept. The exhibition is curated by the distinguished art historian Iris Kritikou. L4A is a proud supporter.
