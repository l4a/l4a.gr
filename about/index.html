About

L4A is an architectural lighting firm based in Athens, Greece. Simplicity is the key principle underscoring our work.

We distribute products by leading european manufacturers, supporting our clients with high quality lighting design services, custom products and project management.

To us Lighting for Architecture means:

  • Lighting for People: human-centred lighting is aimed at human well-being and not human vanity.
  • Lighting for Nature: energy savings, limiting light pollution and light trespass, glare control, proper use of daylight and optimum engineering are vital elements of an environmentally-friendly lighting installation.
  • Lighting for Art: artificial light as well as daylight can be part of amazing artistic installations.

Our team consists of professionals with considerable experience and a wealth of knowledge in the field of architectural lighting with a broad portfolio of projects under their belts, ranging from residential projects, work spaces, retail stores, hotels and museums to industrial, urban, amenity, heritage, religious and historical sites and buildings.
