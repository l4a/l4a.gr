About

L4A is an Athens Greece based Architectural Lighting firm. Simplicity is the key principle of our work.

We distribute leading European manufacturers like iGuzzini, Buzzi & Buzzi, Planlicht, Oligo and Royal Botania, supporting our clients with high quality lighting design services, custom products and project management.

Lighting for Architecture to us means,

  • lighting for People: a human centered lighting is aiming to human wellbeing and not human vanity.
  • lighting for Nature: the energy saving, the limitation of light pollution and light trespass, the glare control, the proper use of daylight and the optimum engineering are elements of an environment friendly lighting installation.
  • lighting for Art: artificial light as well as daylight, can be parts of amazing artistic installations.

Our team consists of professionals with experience and knowledge in the field of architectural lighting with a broad portfolio of projects, from residential, over work spaces, retail stores, hotels and museums to industrial, urban, amenity, heritage, religious and historical sites & buildings.
